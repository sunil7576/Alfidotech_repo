# Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance across different product categories, customer segments, regions, and time periods. The analysis helps identify sales trends, top-performing products, and regional performance while providing business recommendations for improving sales.

---

# Dataset Overview

* **Dataset Name:** Superstore Sales Dataset
* **Tool Used:** Microsoft Power BI
* **Dataset Format:** CSV
* **Analysis Type:** Sales Performance Analysis

---

# Data Preparation

The following preprocessing steps were completed before analysis:

* Imported the dataset into Microsoft Power BI.
* Checked and corrected data types.
* Removed duplicate records.
* Verified missing values.
* Created DAX measures for KPIs.
* Built an interactive dashboard with slicers and charts.

# Dashboard Visualizations

## 1. Sales by Category

A bar chart was created to compare sales across product categories.

**Observation**

* Technology generated the highest sales.
* Furniture ranked second.
* Office Supplies contributed the lowest sales among the three categories.

---

## 2. Sales by Region

A pie chart was used to compare regional sales.

**Observation**

* West region generated the highest sales.
* East region also contributed significantly.
* Central and South regions generated comparatively lower sales.

---

## 3. Sales by Segment

A donut chart was used to analyze customer segments.

**Observation**

* Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.
* Corporate segment ranked second.
* Home Office generated the least sales.

---

## 4. Sales Trend Analysis

A line chart was created to analyze yearly sales performance.

**Observation**

* Sales showed a consistent upward trend over the years.
* Business growth remained positive throughout the analyzed period.

---

## 5. Sales by Sub-Category

A column chart was created to compare product sub-categories.

**Observation**

* Phones generated the highest sales.
* Chairs and Storage also performed well.
* Several sub-categories contributed relatively lower revenue.

---

## 6. Top Products Analysis

A bar chart highlighted the top-selling products.

**Observation**

* A few products generated a significant portion of total revenue.
* These products should receive higher inventory priority.

---

## 7. State-wise Sales Analysis

A map visualization displayed sales distribution across different states.

**Observation**

* Sales are spread across multiple states, with some locations contributing significantly more revenue than others.

---

# Key Business Insights

1. Technology products generate the highest sales revenue.
2. Consumer customers contribute the largest share of total sales.
3. West region performs better than the other regions.
4. Sales have steadily increased over time, indicating healthy business growth.
5. A small number of products contribute a large percentage of total sales.

---

# Business Recommendations

### 1. Increase Inventory

Maintain higher stock levels for top-performing products.

### 2. Expand High-Performing Categories

Invest more in Technology products through promotions and marketing.

### 3. Focus on Regional Growth

Strengthen sales strategies in Central and South regions.

### 4. Customer Retention

Offer loyalty rewards and personalized promotions to Consumer segment customers.

### 5. Improve Product Portfolio

Analyze low-performing products and redesign pricing or promotional strategies.

# Conclusion

The Sales Performance Dashboard provides valuable insights into product performance, customer segments, regional sales, and business growth trends. The findings can help Alfido Tech make data-driven decisions to improve sales performance, customer satisfaction, and future business strategies.
