# Alfido Tech Data Analytics Internship

## Task 2: Sales Performance Analysis

### Project Objective

This project analyzes sales performance using Microsoft Power BI to identify business trends, product performance, regional sales distribution, and customer segments.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* DAX
* Power Query
* CSV Dataset

---

## Dashboard KPIs

* Total Sales: 2.26M
* Total Customers: 793
* Total Orders: 5K
* Average Sales: 230.77

---

## Dashboard Features

* Sales by Category
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Segment
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Top Selling Products
* State-wise Sales Map
* Interactive Slicers

---

## Key Insights

* Technology is the highest-performing category.
* Consumer is the largest customer segment.
* West region generated the highest sales.
* Sales increased steadily over time.
* Top products contribute significantly to overall revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory for top-selling products.
* Focus marketing on Technology category.
* Improve sales in low-performing regions.
* Strengthen customer loyalty programs.
* Optimize low-performing product strategies.
