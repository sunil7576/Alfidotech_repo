# Employee Attrition Analysis for Alfido Tech

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover and provide actionable recommendations for improving employee retention at Alfido Tech.

The analysis was performed using Power BI and focuses on employee demographics, job satisfaction, overtime, department-wise attrition, and workforce trends.

---

## Objective

To understand employee attrition patterns and identify factors contributing to employee turnover.

---

## Dataset Information

Dataset: Employee Attrition Dataset

Total Records: 1470 Employees

Key Features:
- Attrition
- Age
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Monthly Income
- Job Satisfaction
- Environment Satisfaction
- Overtime
- Years at Company

---

## Data Cleaning

The following data preparation steps were performed:

- Imported CSV dataset into Power BI
- Checked for missing values
- Verified data types
- Reviewed duplicate records
- Created custom measures and KPIs

---

## KPIs Created

- Total Employees
- Employees Left
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age
- Average Monthly Income

## Dashboard Visualizations

---

## Key Findings

- Employee attrition rate is approximately 16%.
- Employees working overtime show higher attrition.
- Lower job satisfaction is associated with employee turnover.
- Certain departments experience higher attrition rates.
- Some job roles have significantly higher employee exits.

---

## Recommendations

1. Reduce excessive overtime workload.
2. Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
3. Improve employee engagement programs.
4. Provide career growth opportunities.
5. Develop retention strategies for high-risk departments.
