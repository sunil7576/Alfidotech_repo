# ALFIDO TECH INTERNSHIP

## Task 1: Customer Behavior Analysis

### Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer transactions and purchasing behavior to identify customer segments, understand purchase patterns, and provide recommendations that can improve customer engagement and retention.

---

## Dataset Information

* Dataset: Customer Behavior Analysis
* Tool Used: Microsoft Power BI
* Records: Approximately 49,000 Customers

---

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI                     | Value       |
| ----------------------- | ----------- |
| Total Revenue           | 552 Million |
| Total Customers         | 49K         |
| Total Returns           | 101K        |
| Average Purchase Amount | 2.73K       |

---

# Dashboard Visualizations

### 1. Product Category Sales Analysis

A bar chart was used to analyze revenue generated by different product categories.

**Observation:**

* Home category generated the highest revenue.
* Electronics and Clothing categories also performed well.

---

### 2. Purchase Trend Analysis

A line chart was created to analyze purchase trends over time.

**Observation:**

* Sales remained stable between 2020 and 2022.
* Revenue declined in 2023.

---

### 3. Payment Method Distribution

A pie chart was used to analyze customer payment preferences.

**Observation:**

* PayPal is the most preferred payment method.
* Credit Card and Cash transactions are nearly equal.

---

### 4. Customer Segment Analysis

Customers were classified into High Value, Medium Value, and Low Value segments.

**Observation:**

* High Value customers contribute significantly to overall revenue.
* Medium and Low Value customers require additional engagement.

---

### 5. Age Group Analysis

Customers were grouped into Young, Middle Age, and Senior categories.

**Observation:**

* Middle-aged customers are the most active buyers.

---

# Key Findings

1. Home products generate maximum revenue.
2. High-value customers contribute the majority of sales.
3. PayPal is the most preferred payment method.
4. Revenue has shown a decline in recent years.
5. Return count is relatively high and requires attention.

---

# Recommendations

### 1. Introduce a Loyalty Program

Provide rewards and exclusive offers to high-value customers to increase retention.

### 2. Focus on Best-Selling Categories

Increase inventory and marketing efforts for Home and Electronics categories.

### 3. Re-engage Low Value Customers

Offer discounts and personalized recommendations.

### 4. Reduce Product Returns

Improve product quality and customer service.

### 5. Increase Customer Retention

Implement email marketing campaigns and personalized promotions.

---

# Conclusion

Customer behavior analysis revealed that high-value customers and Home category products are the major contributors to revenue. Strategic marketing efforts, customer retention programs, and product quality improvements can help Alfido Tech improve customer engagement and business performance.
